**1. Main Points**
- Building a multimodal AI product for startup founders
- Users upload a product photo → AI generates marketing copy + social ad
- Users can share voice notes → AI summarizes and gives action items
- Using OpenAI’s Vision, transcription, and text-to-speech APIs
- MVP targeted for launch in 30 days

**2. Product Opportunities**
- Fast creation of quality marketing content from product photos
- Streamlined flow from voice brainstorming to actionable task lists
- Reduce need for founders to multitask or juggle multiple tools
- Automate ad creation to save time and improve professionalism

**3. User Pain Points**
- Startups lack time and resources to create marketing materials
- Non-technical founders may struggle with design/writing ads
- Team meetings often result in unclear action items
- Switching between tools slows down workflows

**4. MVP Idea**
- Web app (or no-code tool) where users:
    - Upload a product photo → instantly get ad copy/social post
    - Record a voice note → receive a summary and action list
- Uses OpenAI APIs:
    - Vision (image to text/copy)
    - Whisper (voice-to-text transcription)
    - GPT (summary and task generation)
    - TTS for spoken summaries (optional)

**5. Revenue Model**
- Freemium: free tier with limits; premium for higher usage
- Subscription for unlimited content/ad generation (monthly/yearly)
- Per-use credit pack for early-stage/test users
- B2B: Team subscriptions for small agencies/startup incubators

**6. Action Items for Students/Founders**
- Research OpenAI Vision, Whisper, and GPT APIs; scope out integration
- Sketch simple Figma wireframes (product photo upload, voice note page)
- Map user flows and define MVP features clearly
- Set up web app stack (e.g., React, Node.js, or use no-code tools)
- Mock data: collect sample product images, voice notes for testing
- Define pricing tiers and soft launch outreach plan
- Set a 30-day project schedule: build, test, feedback, iterate
